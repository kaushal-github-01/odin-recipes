Pork Schnitzel

This mustard-crusted salmon is a wonderful way to prepare fresh salmon fillets in the oven. Be sure to make extra — your family will be begging for more!

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup chopped celery
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine tuna, mayonnaise, celery, onion, parsley, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; mix well. Season with paprika; refrigerate until chilled.
